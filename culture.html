Malaysian Fun Facts / Culture

Malaysia is located in Southeastern Asia, close to Thailand, Indonesia, & Singapore.

Malaysia uses an alphabet language such as ABC, and our second language is English, so it is not that hard to speak over there :).

In Malaysia, the government was ruled by a King and Queen for a long time.

With constitutional changes, the country now has a King and Queen but is governed by a Prime Minister.

The Malaysian Flag and the United States are closely similar.

As you can see in the photo above, the Malaysia flag looks similar to the United States, and the stripes also have a similar meaning.

In the United States, we have 13 stripes for the original colonies, and in Malaysia, there are 13 stripes for the equality of the 13 member states.

Malaysia Has an Old Traditional Hobby

Let me present to you all "WAU KITES," in other names; MOON KITES.

WAU Kites have been passed down for many generations in Malaysia, and they are the most fun and amazing type of kites that are built with Bamboo sticks.

This type of kite is so amazing that when the wind blows, you can hear a sound as the wind goes through them, and it even sounds like "WAU."
